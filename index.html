ManPower Services

Prism Manpower Services is your one-stop-shop for all manpower as well as real estate requirements. Backed by a dedicated team of professionals, we specialize in offering services for Event Management, Facility Management, Manpower Recruitment, Corporate Staffing, and Real Estate. Prism Manpower Services is a premier HR Consultant and HR management consultant, committed to performance, quality and results. We strive to exceed the expectations of our clients.

In a demanding business with high expectations and standards, we honestly represent talented candidates to the clients and our clients to the candidates. We do not promise what we cannot deliver, therefore we accord the highest respect to information that we have been entrusted with. Our main goal is to furnish our clients with manpower talent they need and when they need it. We have always believed the philosophy of quality and our motto is to make the customers delightful with "Total Satisfaction" through personal, quality and professional service.

Staffing Solutions

Located in Mumbai, the company is successfully serving several companies all over the country with comprehensive Staffing Solutions. Owing to our well-knitted network across the nation, we are capable to meet the requirements for staff for several companies.

From fresher to experienced, and young to old, we can successfully meet the requirements of the companies within the committed time. We understand that every company has its own business policies and terms & conditions; therefore, to respect that, we work as per them and offer them the Staffing Solutions that best caters their requirements.

Event Management Services

Any event needs a long planning, preparation and scheduling right from the very beginning. In fact, these are the factors, which help in the smooth running of an event. Events like birthdays, family gatherings, weddings and official events like award shows, product endorsement and launch all needs a detailed planning even if it is a small event or a big one.

We offer our comprehensive Event Management Services for Wedding party, birthday party, award ceremonies, fashion show, musical concerts, opening & inauguration, conference & seminars, exhibitions, etc.

Before planning an event, the first and foremost thing, which should be kept in mind, is the budget limitations. Budget is what matters most the most and before organizing any event, we always understand the budget limit of the clients. The secret behind the success of an event lies in understanding the needs and desires of the guests and besides numerous other reasons like planning on time, understanding the clients’ requirement and so on.
